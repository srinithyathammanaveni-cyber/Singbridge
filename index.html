HOW IT WORKS

FOUR STEPS TO SIGN

01
Camera Capture

Your webcam streams live at ~20fps. No data is stored — everything runs locally via localhost.

02
MediaPipe Detection

21 hand landmarks are extracted per frame using Google's MediaPipe model, supporting up to 2 hands.

03
AI Classification

Claude AI interprets the landmark geometry and identifies the ASL gesture with a confidence score.

04
Text & Speech

Confirmed gestures build a sentence automatically and can be read aloud in English, Hindi, or Telugu.
